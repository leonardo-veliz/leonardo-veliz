About Leonardo

Leonardo Véliz is a versatile music producer, composer, and multi-instrumentalist with a rich background in various musical disciplines. His passion lies in crafting unique and impactful sound experiences across diverse genres.

Musical Foundations

His extensive musical journey began with rigorous formal training at a conservatory, where he dedicated over 12 years to drum studies and more than 11 years to trumpet mastery. He consistently achieved top marks (Grade 1+) in his annual examinations for both instruments, building a strong foundation in classical and jazz disciplines.

Leonardo boasts a rich performance history, having graced stages in renowned venues like the Jazzclub Karlsruhe. He has broad band experience spanning genres including Rock, Pop, Jazz, and Funk, showcasing his versatility on keys, drums, and trumpet.

Collaborations & Production

He also co-founded the "Véliz-Quintett" with other talented young jazz musicians from Karlsruhe. This ensemble has performed at significant events such as the HKA (University) and the "Das Fest" pre-festival. His talent was further recognized when he was spontaneously recruited by esteemed musician Peter Lehel to play trumpet in the Big Band of the Hochschule für Musik Karlsruhe for two notable gigs, including one in Baden-Baden.

For over 5.5 years, Leonardo has been deeply immersed in music production, mastering various software and techniques across a diverse range of genres. He has successfully collaborated with artists, including Nelita, bringing their artistic visions to life through innovative sound design and production.

Leonardo is always open to new ideas and creative partnerships to develop innovative sounds together.
